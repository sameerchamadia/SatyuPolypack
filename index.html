Our Manufacturing Process

We follow a systematic approach to ensure consistent quality in every product

1

Raw Material Selection

We source premium quality LLDPE, LDPE, and HDPE resins from trusted suppliers to ensure product durability.

2

Extrusion & Printing

Advanced extrusion machines convert raw materials into film, followed by high-quality printing for branding.

3

Bag Making

Automated bag-making machines convert printed film into finished bags with precise dimensions and sealing.

4

Quality Control

Each batch undergoes rigorous testing for thickness, strength, seal integrity, and print quality.

5

Packaging & Dispatch

Products are carefully packed and dispatched through our efficient logistics network to clients worldwide.
